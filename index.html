* ABOUT THE GAME *

! Contains mild body horror of animals and people. Discretion is advised.

"Protect the people of Espacio!"

Sheriff Looper is a western horror game where your wife gets abducted and you must find her at any cost. As the appointed Sheriff of Espacio, use your looping lasso to help herd runaway bulls, catch pests, and handle mysterious threats. This is a standalone title - not related to the Hungry Lamu series, despite sharing the same developer.

Sheriff Looper features looping mechanics similar to Pokemon Ranger, taking approximately 30 minutes to complete. Built with the Godot Engine, Sheriff Looper represents developer kulu's vision of blending Western aesthetics with subtle horror elements in beautiful pixel art style. This is kulu's last game of 2025, expanded from the GMTK Game Jam 2025 entry "Looper's Roundup."

Don't be fooled by Sheriff Looper's charming pixel art style - players have noted the game's clever bait-and-switch, describing it as "a nice wholesome pixel art style to lure you in... until the incident." The dev does well with body horror and knows how to tell a good story. Western isn't too common for horror, and Sheriff Looper delivers another solid narrative experience.

Looper's Roundup!

Sheriff Looper began as a game jam entry called "Looper's Roundup!" created for the GMTK Game Jam 2025. The original jam version focused on helping a local farmer round up runaway bulls using a trusty lasso.

TIPS & TRICKS

01

Master the Lasso

In Sheriff Looper, your lasso is your main tool. Practice aiming and timing to catch moving targets efficiently.

02

Explore Everything

The town of Espacio hides many secrets. Talk to everyone and check every corner.

03

Watch for Signs

The horror elements are subtle. Pay attention to environmental changes and audio cues.

04

Take Your Time

Sheriff Looper is a ~30 minute experience. Don't rush - immerse yourself in the atmosphere.
